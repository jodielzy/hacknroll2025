How to Play

Tutorial

This is a training game that teaches you the basic shortcuts. Follow along the instructions shown on your screen and try pressing the shortcuts as instructed. If you get stuck, you can press the "Hint" button to get guidance on what shortcut to use.

Race

Compete with your friends to see who is faster at mastering Windows shortcuts! Join a room by entering a name for yourself and a room name. Once both players are ready, the game will start. Each player must press the correct shortcut for the task shown on the screen. The winner is the one who completes all tasks the fastest!
